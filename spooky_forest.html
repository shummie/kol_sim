Level 2 Quest: The Spooky Forest

Quest Description

The main goal of this quest is to obtain a mosquito larva from the Spooky Forest in the non-combat adventure located here.

However, realistically, you will be spending additional time in this zone to prep for the Hidden City Unlock (Hidden Temple Quest). This involves the acquisition of a spooky sapling, a tree-holed coin which is then traded for a spooky temple map, and then the spooky-gro fertilizer.
Note, that the spooky-gro fertilizer can be pulled, analysis showing turns saved will be explored on this page.

All analysis on this page is going to be related to the total number of turns taken to both complete the council quest along with the parts needed for the hidden city/temple unlock.

Analysis

Completing this quest really boils down to encountering the Arboreal Respire non-combat adventure a sufficient number of times.

1 to get the mosquito larva.
3 to get the spooky sapling, coin, and map.
1 more for the spooky gro fertilizer (separated for the analysis to determine pull savings).

As a result, maximizing non-combat frequency is the key to speeding up this zone.

There are a few additional things to note for this zone.

Delay

There is a 5 turn delay (0-indexed) before the non-combat is eligible to be placed in the queue.

Superlikely

There is a superlikely version of the non-combat adventure which forces the appearance of the adventure after 7 or 8 turns (equal chance) since you last encountered either version of the non-combat.

The combat rate is 85%.

Strategy

Running at a capped -25% Combat modifier is the main way to complete the quest as quickly as possible.
With the fact that there exists a 5 turn delay on the appearance of the non-combat adventure, utilize free fights / runaways to reduce the delay counter in this zone.
